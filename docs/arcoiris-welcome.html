Fan Capture Strategy / Cómo Usar el QR Code

At Shows & Events

  • Print the QR code on large banners (minimum 30x30 cm)
  • Place it visibly at the entrance and on stage
  • Announce: “Scan the code for a FREE tango class!”
  • Every person who scans becomes a registered fan

On Social Media

  • Add the link to your Instagram bio
  • Share the link in your stories with “Swipe up”
  • Post the QR code as an image in your posts
  • Use in Facebook/Instagram ads

On Printed Materials

  • Business cards with QR code
  • Event flyers
  • Posters at tango academies
  • Menus at partner restaurants

Metrics to Monitor

  • Total fans captured (see your portal → FRM)
  • QR conversion rate
  • Most-used devices (iPhone vs Android)
  • Cities where your fans come from
