## ❤️ The Mission

**"We don't just sign artists. We turn them into companies."**

Apex Music Latino exists to give Latin artists the infrastructure that major labels kept for themselves — the data, the tools, the legal clarity, the marketing machine, the AI agents — and give it back to the artist. Fully owned. Forever.

---

## 🌎 The Problem We Solve

The music industry has always been rigged. Legacy labels:
- **Kept the data** (fan emails, streams, purchase history)
- **Kept the masters** (publishing rights, sync licensing)
- **Kept the money** (opaque accounting that killed Death Row, Ruthless, and thousands more)

Latin artists — especially in Colombia, Mexico, Argentina — are **world-class talent** trapped in **world-class exploitation**.

CUBO changes that.

---

## 🔮 The Vision (3-Year Horizon)

1. **Artist OS** — Every artist has a portal. Their own dashboard. Their own data. Their own brand.
2. **CUBO FRM** — A fan relationship engine that unifies signals from Spotify, Instagram, TikTok, WhatsApp, QR codes, and event check-ins into a single fan profile. Owned by the artist. Backed up forever.
3. **The Pack (AI Agents)** — Six specialized AI bots (Kujo, Benji, Fido, Roxy, Scout, Nextel) that replace a full management team. Available at Gold, Silver, and Bronze tiers.
4. **Apex Academy** — The knowledge layer. Contracts 101, royalties, branding, digital distribution. What the industry never taught artists on purpose.
5. **Marketplace** — Brands find artists by data, not by gut feeling. Artists monetize on their terms.

---

## 🎯 The Moat

**FRM + Data Ownership is the moat.**

When an artist gets hacked, banned, or dropped from a platform — they lose everything. With CUBO:
- Their fans are backed up in a sovereign database
- Their contact list is theirs, not Instagram's
- Their engagement history is portable
- Their QR scan history is logged and auditable

> "Own your data forever. Extract it from social media before you get hacked and lose your fans." — Core Value Proposition

## 🐾 The Pack Philosophy

The Apex AI Agents are named after dogs because **great dogs are loyal, relentless, and specialized**:

- **Kujo** hunts. He doesn't rest until the booking is closed.
- **Benji** counts. Every dollar in, every dollar out, tracked and reported.
- **Fido** guards. Fiduciary duty — he watches what lawyers should watch.
- **Roxy** performs. Image, PR, EPK — she makes the artist look premium always.
- **Scout** finds. Venue heat maps, genre trends, the next wave before it breaks.
- **Nextel** connects. The chirp between agents and the artist. "You've got a hot lead."

> Each agent consumes AI tokens (Gold > Silver > Bronze). This is the monetization engine.
